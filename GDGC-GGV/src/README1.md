## 🔭 Overview
The GDGC Website redesign is a structured, team-based initiative to modernize the digital presence of the Google Developer Group on Campus chapter at Guru Ghasidas Vishwavidyalaya (GGV). By adopting a mobile-first and responsive approach, the project transforms community requirements into a high-fidelity digital experience. The deliverables include an interactive Figma prototype and a fully developed application, ensuring a cohesive user journey for all community members.

---

## ⚠️ Problem Statement
The previous GDGC website lacked a modern, responsive, and user-friendly interface. Users experienced several pain points:
* **Poor Navigation:** An unclear information hierarchy made it difficult to browse different sections.
* **Low Discoverability:** Crucial information regarding events, community activities, and resources was hard to find.
* **Outdated Aesthetics:** The design lacked visual engagement and failed to represent the community's vibrant identity.
* **Disorganized Content:** Unstructured layouts hindered quick access to relevant information.

**Goal:** Redesign the platform to feature improved UI/UX, intuitive navigation, responsive layouts, and interactive elements to boost overall engagement.

---

## ✨ Features
* 📱 **Fully Responsive Design:** Optimized for seamless usage across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
* 🎨 **Culturally Integrated UI:** Incorporates a unique "Digital Bastar" identity, blending regional art with modern tech aesthetics.
* 📅 **Dynamic Event Management:** Dedicated tabs for live/upcoming and past events, complete with category filters and registration links.
* 👥 **Interactive Team Directory:** Matrix filters and dynamic data lists to easily explore core team members and departments.
* 📝 **Streamlined Onboarding:** A user-friendly, multi-step application form with integrated resume upload.
* 🔍 **High Discoverability:** Bento grid layouts and clear visual hierarchies ensure content is scannable and accessible.

## 📂 Website Pages

### 🏠 Home
The primary landing section designed to create a strong first impression.
* **Hero Section:** High-energy headline ("Coding the Future Rooted in Innovation"), community mission, and an "Innovation Tree" illustration.
* **Featured Events:** Bento grid style cards for upcoming activities.
* **Community Statistics:** Dynamic counters for members and events.

### ℹ️ About
A deep dive into the purpose, goals, and identity of GDGC GGV.
* **Mission & Vision:** Clear statement of the community's purpose.
* **Core Pillars:** Three-column layout focusing on *Events & Workshops*, *Learning & Growth*, and *Projects & Innovation*.
* **Timeline & Benefits:** Showcasing student value, networking, and skill-building opportunities.

### 🎟️ Events
Designed to manage and showcase all community activities.
* **Event Navigation:** Toggle tabs to switch between "Live/Upcoming" and "Past" events.
* **Featured Cards:** High-contrast displays for major current events (e.g., Google Solutions Challenge).
* **Archive & Gallery:** Grid layouts displaying past session memories.

### 🤝 Team
Highlights the core members, executives, and overall organizational structure.
* **Core Organizers:** Profile grid with circular avatars and LinkedIn shortcuts.
* **Department Filter:** Matrix bar enabling users to view distinct departments (e.g., Tech, Design, Management).
* **Skill Interactivity:** Profiles displaying technical expertise and roles upon interaction.

### 🚀 Join Community
A dedicated portal for users to apply and become members.
* **Multi-Step Wizard:** A frictionless horizontal stepper dividing the process into:
    1. Personal Information & Interests
    2. Contact Details
    3. Resume Upload (Drag-and-Drop)
* **Success State:** Welcoming confirmation message upon submission.

### 📞 Contact
Provides accessible communication channels for inquiries and support.
* **FAQ Accordion:** Answers to common student queries.
* **Contact Form:** Minimalist form for direct messaging.
* **Campus Location & Socials:** Physical address with map integration, official email, phone numbers, and social media links.

## 🛠️ Design Process

### 1. Research Phase
Analyzed leading developer community platforms (GDSC Algiers, GDG Chennai, GDG Casablanca) to define strategic foundations. The research established the need for a high-energy hero section, scannable Bento Grids, 3-pillar structures for immediate student value, and 2-step forms to reduce user fatigue.

### 2. Wireframing
Developed low-fidelity wireframes using Visily to map out the overall page structure, establishing a clear site hierarchy for the six core pages.

### 3. Architecture
Structured the information flow focusing on modular dashboard cards, mission-driven layouts, and accessibility-focused UX. Created detailed flowcharts and site maps.

### 4. UI Design
Designed high-fidelity, responsive screens in Figma. Established a robust design system ensuring visual consistency across all touchpoints.

### 5. Prototyping
Built a fully interactive prototype featuring seamless page transitions, hover effects, and realistic user flows to simulate the final product environment.

### 6. Testing
Conducted rigorous evaluations focusing on:
* Navigation efficiency
* Responsive behavior across devices
* Visual consistency
* User accessibility and interface usability

## 🎨 Design System
The visual language bridges modern minimalist UI with regional cultural aesthetics.

* **Style:** Minimal dashboard UI, rounded cards, Bento-grid layouts, soft shadows.
* **Typography & Colors:** High-contrast, modern typography with a consistent, energetic color palette.
* **Digital Bastar Identity:** Integrated regional elements to provide a unique local feel, including:
    * Dhokra geometric patterns
    * Tribal separators
    * Tree of Life visuals
    * Regional cultural motifs and animal morph-style graphics
